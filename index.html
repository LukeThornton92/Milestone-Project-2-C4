Rules

  • Setup:
    • The game is played on a vertical board with 7 columns and 6 rows.
    • Each player chooses a color (red or yellow).
  • Objective:
    • The goal is to be the first to get four of your colored pieces in a row.
  • Turns:
    • Players take turns dropping one of their colored pieces into any of the columns.
    • The piece will fall to the lowest available space within the column.
  • Winning:
    • You win by connecting four of your pieces in a row.
    • The four pieces can be in a straight line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Draw:
    • If the board is completely filled and no player has four in a row, the game is a draw.
  • Starting a New Game:
    • After a win or a draw, the board is cleared and you can start a new game.
